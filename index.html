About me

I'm a passionate UX Designer seeking to support companies to provide their clients with a thoughtful experience.

For more than eight years of experience as an HR professional, I helped NGOs attract high-qualified talents by adapting processes to the needs of people. It was natural for me to make the move to user experience design as the focus of my work have always been the needs of others. My expertise in communication and interviewing helps me to find points of friction.

I’m excited to expand my research and design-thinking skills, and to uncover the most desirable solutions for your users.
