About me

I'm a passionate UX Designer seeking to support digital agencies and IT companies to provide their clients with thoughtful experiences.

For more than eight years, I helped NGOs attract high-qualified talents. I value impactful work, open collaboration, and a spirit of diversity and inclusion. It was natural for me to transition from HR to UX Design. The deep desire to understand people and find solutions to meet their needs has always been the focus of my work.

My expertise in communication and interviewing helps me to find points of friction. With a hands-on approach combined with my design-thinking skills, I can remove obstacles to uncover the most desirable solutions for your users.
